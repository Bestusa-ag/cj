Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Communicate Well.

What do I need to start learning?

Just a web browser (like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari) on a computer, tablet, or phone. You don't need to download or install anything!

How is my learning progress saved?

The list of courses you've completed is saved right on your device in the browser. It is private to you and not shared with anyone.

Can I reset my learning history?

Yes! If you want to start over, you can clear your browser's site data for our website. Check your browser's help section for instructions.

How do I navigate the courses?

Our courses use simple video controls. You can also use keyboard shortcuts for play/pause. Each course will have specific instructions if needed.

Will the courses work on my device?

Our courses are designed to work well on most modern devices, from desktops to smartphones. You should have a smooth and effective learning experience.
