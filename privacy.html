Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Our Promise

Communicate Well is made for learning. We don't require accounts or ask for personal information to access courses. Your privacy is our priority.

What Info We Use

  • Learning Progress: Your course completion history is saved on your own device in your browser. We do not have access to it.
  • Contact Form: If you send us a message, we'll use your email only to reply to your inquiry.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. You can access all our courses without them.

Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser's site data to erase your learning history and start fresh.
  • Using a private or incognito window in your browser means your learning history won't be saved after you close it.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please use the contact form on our home page.
